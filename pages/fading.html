fading frontier

"On August 16, 2015, Deerhunter released a video for a new song titled "Snakeskin," and announced that their new album, Fading Frontier, was released on October 16, 2015, on the 4AD label. The song featured alto saxophonist Zumi Rosow and the video was directed by Valentina Tapia. On 13 October, Deerhunter shared the video for 'Living My Life', dedicated to Miles Davis. The LP was very well received by critics." -wikipedia
